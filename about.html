About your provider

I built this clinic because women in their 40s deserve better than a 10-minute visit.

I'm a nurse practitioner with a background in primary care and wellness. I started Thryve because I watched too many women leave appointments with their concerns minimized, their labs called normal, and no real plan. Women who were doing everything right and still felt terrible.

That's the sentence I say in every first visit. My job is to connect your symptoms, history, and labs into something that actually makes sense — and give you a clear plan, in writing, that you can follow.

I believe in treating the whole picture, not just the loudest symptom. Hormones, metabolism, mood, sleep — these systems talk to each other, and a good plan accounts for all of them.

All care is virtual. I'm licensed in Arizona, New Mexico, and North Dakota. Wherever you are in those states, you deserve expert care without the waiting room.
