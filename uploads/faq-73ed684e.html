Common questions

Answers before you book. Still have one? Book a free 15-min discovery call.

My doctor said my labs are normal. Why would this be different?+
Standard reference ranges are built on population averages, not optimal function for your specific symptoms and age. We review your labs in context of how you actually feel — which is a completely different clinical conversation than 'your number is within range.'
Which states do you serve?+
Arizona, New Mexico, and North Dakota. You must be physically located in your state at the time of your visit. All care is virtual — no in-person visits required.
Does insurance cover this?+
We are cash-pay only and do not bill insurance. We can provide a superbill for potential reimbursement. HSA and FSA cards are accepted for memberships and lab panels.
How does the pharmacy work?+
We send Rx to a compounding pharmacy of your choice — primarily Empower Pharmacy or Hallandale Health. You pay the pharmacy directly. Compounded medications are significantly more affordable than brand-name.
Not sure if I need HRT or weight loss support?+
Book a free 15-minute discovery call. Hormones and weight are deeply connected in perimenopause — many women benefit from addressing both. We'll point you in the right direction before you commit to anything.
What happens if I'm not a good fit?+
If your needs fall outside our scope, we'll tell you honestly and point you toward the right next step. We don't take patients we can't genuinely help.
How is my information protected?+
All clinical communication happens through our secure, HIPAA-compliant portal. We don't use social media, standard contact forms, or regular SMS for patient care.
Can I cancel my membership?+
Yes — any time with 30 days notice. No long-term contracts. The onboarding fee covers your initial consult time and is non-refundable.
